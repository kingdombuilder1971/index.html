How to use
Claude

A simple, five-lesson introduction to having your first conversation with an AI assistant.

5 min read For absolute beginners No sign-up required to read
1

What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant made by a company called Anthropic. Think of it as a very knowledgeable conversation partner — you type a question or request, and it writes back a helpful response.

You can use it to get answers, help with writing, learn something new, work through a problem, or just satisfy your curiosity. It responds in plain, natural language — no technical jargon required.

Claude works in your browser at claude.ai — no downloads or installs needed.
2

How to write your first message

Go to claude.ai, sign in, and you'll see a text box at the bottom of the screen. Type your question or request there and press Enter (or click the arrow button).

There's no special format — just write naturally, the way you'd text a friend.

Example message
Can you explain what a spreadsheet is in simple terms?
You don't need to say "please" or use formal language. Plain, conversational writing works best.
3

Be specific for better answers

The more detail you give Claude, the more useful its response will be. If you ask something vague, you'll get a vague answer. Adding a bit of context makes a big difference.

Vague vs. specific
❌  Help me write an email

✅  Help me write a short, friendly email to my manager
    asking for a day off next Friday.
If the first answer isn't quite right, just reply and ask Claude to adjust it. You can say things like "make it shorter" or "use simpler words."
4

You can have a real conversation

Claude remembers everything said earlier in the same conversation. So you can ask follow-up questions, ask it to change something, or go deeper on a topic — without repeating yourself each time.

A back-and-forth example
You: Summarise the water cycle in two sentences.
Claude: [gives summary]
You: Now make it suitable for a 10-year-old.
Claude: [rewrites it in simpler language]
Each new conversation starts fresh — Claude won't remember anything from previous chats.
5

Things Claude can help you with

Claude can assist with a huge range of everyday tasks. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Things to try
• Explain a confusing concept in plain language
• Proofread or improve a piece of writing
• Help plan a trip, event, or project
• Answer questions you're curious about
• Brainstorm ideas
• Help you write or reply to emails
• Explain how something works
Claude isn't always perfect. If an answer seems wrong or confusing, ask it to try again or double-check important information elsewhere.
